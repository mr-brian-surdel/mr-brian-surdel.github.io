About Me

Hello! I'm Brian Surdel I lead the Design and Development department at Blue Magnet Interactive and would characterize my role as a player-manager. I oversee every inch of development processes, starting with sales and ending with deployment and ongoing optimizations. Leading a team of talented people, both in and out of house, I feel proud of the highest quality digital marketing platforms we produce. I have the unique experience of holding a position on the leadership team while also aiding the execution of ground work on day to day projects. I have personally streamlined the development process at BMI, from being the architect of the new budgeting and cost evaluation system, to streamlining development & deployment practices to be devastatingly competitive on the modern web.
